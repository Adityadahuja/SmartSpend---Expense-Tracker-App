## Project Goal
The primary goal of **SmartSpend** is to provide a simple, intuitive, and privacy-focused application for individuals to track their daily income and expenses. It aims to:
- **Simplify Financial Tracking**: Replace complex spreadsheets with an easy-to-use interface.
- **Provide Insights**: Offer visual analytics (charts, graphs) to help users understand their spending habits.
- **Ensure Privacy**: Store data locally to ensure user financial information remains private and secure.
- **Promote Financial Health**: Help users stay within their budget by monitoring their cash flow.
